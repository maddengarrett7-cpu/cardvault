Our Mission

Built for collectors,
by a collector.

CardScan exists to make logging cards accurately faster and easier — so you spend less time on spreadsheets and more time in the hobby.

The problem we solved

If you've ever been at a card show with a stack of slabs and no easy way to log what you paid and what you picked up — you know the pain. Writing it down is slow. Typing it manually is error-prone. By the time you're home, you've already forgotten half the cert numbers.

We built CardScan to fix that. Point your phone at a slab, and the AI reads the player, year, set, grade, and cert number instantly. It logs everything straight to your Google Sheet — no typing required.

What we believe

The hobby shouldn't require a second job in data entry. Whether you're a casual collector tracking your PC or a dealer logging hundreds of cards at a show, your time is better spent on the cards — not the spreadsheet.

CardScan is designed to get out of your way. Open it, scan, done. Your collection tracks itself.
